Four documents, in order: Purchase Order → GRN → Purchase Bill → Supplier Payment.

⚠️ A Purchase Order is only a request — it does not change stock. Your stock goes up only when you post the GRN.

6 Selling at the POS (retail)

Sales → POS

6.1 Make a sale

  1. Pick the branch/terminal (Main Branch – Gulberg).
  2. Scan barcode 5449000000996 or search “Coca” → it enters the cart at 80.
  3. Set quantity = 2 (total 160).
  4. (Optional) Select customer Ahmed Khan.
  5. Click Pay / Charge → Method Cash → Tendered 200 → Change shows 40.
  6. Click Complete → receipt is generated (and printed if a printer is set up).
✅ Coca-Cola stock 48 → 46. The sale and its profit post to accounting automatically.
💡 Hold / Park a sale: click Hold to park a cart; resume it later from Restaurant → Held Sales (used for retail too).

Close the day

Operations → Daily Closing

Count your cash drawer, enter the amount; the system compares it to expected cash (sales − refunds + payments) and locks the day. Shifts (Operations → Shifts): cashiers open a shift at start and close to reconcile their drawer.

A The complete flow at a glance

SETUP          Branch → Units → Categories
CATALOG        Products (retail + ingredient + menu item)
PEOPLE         Supplier + Customer
BUY            Purchase Order → GRN (stock +) → Purchase Bill → Supplier Payment (cash −)
SELL           POS sale (stock −) → [Return] → Sales Ledger
RESTAURANT     Floor → Table → Waiter → Table Board → KOT → KDS → Settle
KITCHEN        Recipe/BOM → (Production) → Wastage   [selling consumes ingredients]
FINANCE        Chart of Accounts → Cash/Bank → Expense → Customer Payment
               → Trial Balance / P&L / Balance Sheet (auto-reconciled)
REPORTS        Sales / Inventory / Purchases / Restaurant / Kitchen
CLOSE          Shift close → Daily Closing

C Daily routine checklist (after setup)

  1. Open shift (cashier) → Operations → Shifts.
  2. Sell all day at Sales → POS (scan, charge, print).
  3. Receive deliveries as they arrive → Purchasing → GRN.
  4. Record expenses as you pay them → Finance → Expenses.
  5. Close shift, then Operations → Daily Closing (count cash).
  6. Check Reports → Sales and Finance → Profit & Loss before leaving.
